FizzBuzzer

A small javascript app that labels all numbers multiples of 3 as 'Fizz', all multiples of 5 as 'Buzz', and all those multiples of both 3 and 5 as 'FizzBuzz'. The rest of the numbers are listed in their respective numeral.
